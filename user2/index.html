ABOUT US

Success Construction Co.,Ltd., was established in January 2022 in order to enable fast, reliable quality engineering services desired in the sector. Although our company is young, the company founders and employees have many years of experience in theory and practice in station equipment and engineering. Our company provides services to all petroleum companies throughout the country with its expert staff in the complete construction, installation and other technical issues related to fuel stations with international quality and safety standards. We offer technological equipment and quality service to our customers, which are the products of world-renowned companies in the sector and which set the standards in the fuel sector. Guaranteed service of all products and services we sell and install are provided by our company.

OUR POLICY

QUALITY POLICY

To fully and accurately understand the wishes/needs and expectations of our customers. Maximizing customer satisfaction, working to maintain and continuously improve the satisfaction line Planning every work, doing it right and exactly the first time, making it a principle.

OHSAS POLICY

All accidents can and should be prevented. There is always a safer way to do every job. Training is always indispensable to effectively implement the OHSAS 18001 Standard and to create and develop an awareness of individual responsibility for Employee Health and Safety issues.

ENVIRONMENTAL POLICY

To comply with the environmental legislation in our country and to ensure its currency. Identify types of environmental impacts and damages, To minimize pollution and waste and to ensure that our hazardous wastes are disposed of with minimal environmental impact.
